About

The Unicorn

I discovered the field of Human Computer Interaction when I was studying Computer Science in graduate school. I'm fascinated by it ever since. I knew I've found my true calling the very first day I started my job as a UX designer. I love solving challenging and complex problems in a visual way and designing delightful user experiences.

When I'm not designing for work, I love to create illustrations and thinking of fun interactions.
