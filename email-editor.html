Marketo Email Editor

Email Editor is the single most important feature in the entire Marketo product suite. More than 80% of Marketo users are email marketers and they spend the majority of their time inside Marketo Email Editor. In this project, I led the design effort to improve the current email design and editing experience.

The Problem

The existing Marketo email editing experience is rather cumbersome:
  • Customers cannot preview and choose the email templates easily in the app.
  • The email templates are predefined. Users cannot add or remove sections in their emails.
  • Marketo automatically break the email down to many rich-text regions, the user has to manually go into each section and edit the content and styles individually inside a popup.
  • Itâ€™s the best practice that marketers should always send a plain text version of the email. Currently, the text option is buried in the UI. A lot of times, the marketers will forget to update their text version.
  • There is no easy way to preview what the email looks like on a mobile device. During my user research, I found that many marketers will preview the email in the email previewer and then manually resize the browser window.
  • For power users, editing email source code in a popup modal is very inefficient.

The Goal

  • Design a user-friendly email template picking and editing experience.
  • Allow user to change the structure and design of the email easily without the need to modify the code.
  • Provide a better way for users to edit code, plain text version of the email.
  • Allow users to preview the email in both desktop and mobile view.

The Challenge

This project is selected to be featured during the Marketo User Summit in May 2016. We have a very important mission with tight deadline. As a UX designer, I had to work very closely with the PM and engineers to keep adjusting the project scope and come up the best design solution within the time and resources allowed.

The Result

I presented the final design to a panel of executives and product stakeholders. The design was well-received. It is currently being implemented.
