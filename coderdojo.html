CoderDojo NYC Website Redesign

CoderDojo NYC is non-profit organization hosting free STEAM workshops for youth aged 7-17. I am thrilled to offer my help as a volunteer designer to help them refresh their existing website.

Challenges
  • CoderDojo receives an overwhelming amount of emails from both parents and future volunteers asking questions about how to sign up. Because of the organization is 100% volunteer based company (no full-time staff), they don’t have enough resources to answer such emails.
  • CoderDojo receives an overwhelming amount of emails from both parents and future volunteers asking questions about how to sign up. Because of the organization is 100% volunteer based company (no full-time staff), they don’t have enough resources to answer such emails. Due to the organization’s unique situations, CoderDojo NYC has a rather unique signing up process for events. This process, however, did not get explained clearly on the website.
  • CoderDojo NYC has two newsletters and it was not clearly marked or explained on the website. Parents and volunteers often sign up for the wrong mailing list.
Goals
  • Refresh the website's visual style.
  • Better explain the sign up processes for both parents and volunteers.
  • Reorganize site content to improve findability.
  • Reduce the number of inqury emails.
Results
The stakeholders are satisfied with my service and the final design. This website is currently being developed.
